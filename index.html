FAQs

Do I need a licence to use RemitSo?

Yes, a valid licence that allows you to operate as a money transfer operator in your business jurisdiction

Is RemitSo the right platform for my money transfer business?

Whether you're a new entrant or an established player, RemitSo is designed for any business in the international money transfer space. Our platform offers a comprehensive suite of tools to streamline your operations, enhance security, and accelerate growth.

What sets RemitSo apart from other money transfer software platforms?

RemitSo stands out with its seamless API integrations, robust security features, scalability, and a commitment to compliance. Our platform also empowers you with features like automated customer onboarding and proactive monitoring, giving you a competitive edge.

Is RemitSo a white-label money transfer software solution?

Absolutely! RemitSo is fully white-labelled, allowing you to customize the platform with your branding and tailor it to your unique business requirements.

Can RemitSo integrate with my existing systems and third-party services?

Yes, RemitSo seamlessly integrates with a wide range of third-party systems, including banks, payment gateways, and other financial institutions. Our extensive API library ensures smooth data exchange and efficient processes.

Does RemitSo offer a mobile app for my customers?

Yes, RemitSo provides customizable mobile applications for iOS and Android, enabling your customers to send and receive money conveniently on their smartphones.

How does RemitSo ensure compliance with regulations like AML and KYC?

RemitSo has robust compliance features built-in, including KYC/AML checks, transaction monitoring, and reporting. We help you stay ahead of regulatory requirements and mitigate risks.
