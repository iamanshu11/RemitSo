FAQs

How quickly can I integrate RemitSo with my Neobank platform?

RemitSo is designed for rapid integration, allowing Neobanks to launch cross-border remittance services in as little as 30 days.

Our API-first architecture and comprehensive documentation streamline the process, minimizing development time. We offer pre-built integrations and support throughout the integration phase, ensuring a smooth and efficient deployment.

What APIs does RemitSo offer for integration?

RemitSo provides a robust suite of RESTful APIs that enable seamless integration with your existing platform. These APIs cover all aspects of cross-border payments, including:

  • Transaction Initiation: APIs for creating and managing international transfers.
  • FX Rates: Real-time exchange rate APIs to provide competitive pricing.
  • KYC/AML: APIs for identity verification and compliance checks.
  • Account Management: APIs for managing customer accounts and balances.
  • Transaction Tracking: APIs for providing real-time transaction status updates.
  • Reporting and Analytics: APIs for accessing transaction data and generating reports.
  • Partner ecosystem:APIs that enable connections to other fintech solutions.

How does RemitSo handle regulatory compliance?

RemitSo understands the importance of regulatory compliance in the financial industry. Our platform incorporates robust KYC/AML checks, including identity verification, anti-forgery measures, risk scoring, and watchlist monitoring.

We stay up-to-date with evolving regulatory requirements and ensure that our platform adheres to the highest standards. We provide automated compliance tools and reporting features to help you manage your regulatory obligations efficiently.

Can I customize the user experience for my customers?

Yes, absolutely. RemitSo's API-first approach allows for extensive customization of the user experience. You can integrate our remittance services seamlessly into your Neobank's existing interface, maintaining a consistent brand experience. Our APIs provide the flexibility to tailor the user flow, branding, and messaging to match your Neobank's unique style.
