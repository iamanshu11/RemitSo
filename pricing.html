FAQs

Estimate monthly fees in the PaaS model based on volume-based pricing?

RemitSo, a volume based pricing model is designed to ensure, costumes only pay for what they use, and with higher transaction volumes cost should go down. 

For examples if, your transaction volume is:

15,000 x £0.082 = £1,230

25,000 x £0.049 = £1,225

35,000 x £0.041 = £1,435

45,000 x £0.033 = £1,485

55,000 x £0.025 = £1,375

75,000 x £0.016 = £1,200

Our tiered pricing model ensures that higher transaction volumes result in greater savings.

Are there any other fees besides the transaction fees?

There is one time set up fee of £3999 (Essential) & £4999 (Momentum) and equivalent amount in different currencies. After that, you pay as you use based on our volume-based pricing model. 

In the Momentum plan, you get 12,000 transactions free for the first year. From the second year onwards, you pay as you use based on our volume-based pricing model.

Does volume-based pricing apply to the Enterprise or Bespoke models?

No, volume-based pricing does not apply to our Enterprise or Bespoke models. These models are tailored to the specific needs of large businesses.

What kind of website and mobile app templates are included in the Essential and Momentum plan?

The Essential plan offers fixed website and mobile app templates. These templates can be personalized with your company's logo and color scheme.

Where is my website and app hosted with the Essential & Momentum plan?

Your website and apps will be hosted on dedicated AWS servers. This is an additional cost to the plan.

Can I get additional services like AML support or account management with the Essential & Momentum plan?

Yes, on-demand value-added services (such as AML Support, AMC, and others) are available at an agreed additional cost.
